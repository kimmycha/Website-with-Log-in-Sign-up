Tradition in Every Flavor

あらゆる味における伝統

Hon-Cha emerged with a mission to honor Japan's culinary legacy. Rooted in tradition, the brand meticulously sources ingredients and employs time-honored techniques to craft its teas and snacks. From fragrant sencha to crispy nori snacks, each product is a tribute to Japanese flavors, inviting consumers on a journey of authenticity and nostalgia. As Hon-Cha grows, its dedication to preserving Japan's rich culinary heritage remains unwavering, ensuring that tradition endures in every sip and bite.

From Leaf to Table

葉からテーブルまで

Our brand Hon-Cha, prioritizes authenticity and quality. We meticulously cultivate our own matcha leaves, handpicking and processing them for a vibrant, flavorful brew. Every dish on the menu reflects a commitment to tradition, sourcing local, seasonal ingredients and employing time-honored cooking techniques. Hon-Cha offers more than just a meal—it's an immersion into Japan's culinary heritage, served with pride and passion.
